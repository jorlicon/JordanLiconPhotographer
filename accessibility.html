Accessibility Statement

Jordan Licon Photography is committed to making the website usable and accessible for visitors with diverse needs and assistive technologies.

Ongoing improvements

The site uses semantic HTML, descriptive image text where practical, readable contrast, responsive layouts, and keyboard-accessible links and controls. Accessibility will continue to be improved as the site evolves.

Feedback

If you have difficulty accessing any part of the website, email info@jordanliconphotography.com with the page URL and a short description of the issue.
