- Architecture Photography Services

Photography for architects, developers, and commercial property teams.

Jordan Licon is an El Paso architectural photographer creating polished architecture photography, interior photography, commercial real estate photography, drone architecture photography, and construction progress photography for projects across El Paso, Texas, New Mexico, and the Southwest.

The work is built for portfolios, award submissions, leasing campaigns, proposals, press, social media, and institutional communications where the built environment has to feel clear, intentional, and valuable.

Architectural stills

Exterior elevations, interior spaces, amenities, material details, and complete property stories delivered with clean color and controlled perspective.

Aerial + site context

FAA Part 107 drone coverage for site relationships, access, surrounding context, rooflines, campuses, and property-scale marketing.

Motion walkthroughs

Short architectural films, leasing reels, and cinematic walkthroughs for spaces that need more atmosphere than a still gallery can carry.

Construction reporting

Progress documentation, repeat-visit photography, stakeholder updates, and optional mapping deliverables for active construction sites.
