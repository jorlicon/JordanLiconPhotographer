06 / Headshot FAQ

Common questions.

What does the $350 session include?

The session includes your studio headshot shoot, direction, 3 outfits, proofing, and 1 finalized headshot.

How much are additional finalized headshots?

Additional finalized headshots are $200 each. You choose them after reviewing your proof gallery.

Do you photograph actors and pageant titleholders?

Yes. Actor and pageantry headshots are available with expression coaching, styling guidance, and polished final retouching.

Can you photograph our team at our office?

Yes. Corporate and team headshots can be photographed in studio or on location, depending on team size, schedule, and visual requirements.
