Why Executive Headshots Matter

Your professional headshot is often the first impression potential clients, partners, and stakeholders have of you. An executive portrait signals professionalism, trustworthiness, and leadership presence.

Professional headshots are used for:

  • LinkedIn profiles - Stand out in your network with a polished, approachable image
  • Corporate websites - Build credibility on your company's leadership page
  • Marketing materials - Use in press releases, email signatures, and branding
  • Speaking engagements - Professional image for conference websites and promotional materials
  • Networking events - Make a strong impression at industry gatherings

Executive Headshot FAQ

Q: What should I wear to my executive headshot session?

A: Wear solid colors or subtle patterns that complement your skin tone. Business attire is standard—think what you'd wear to a client meeting. We recommend 2-3 outfit options so you can choose the looks you prefer.

Q: Can I get different hairstyles or looks in one session?

A: Yes. Three outfit changes are included in every session. Some clients bring different blazers, shirts, or sweaters to show versatility.

Q: How will my headshot look on LinkedIn and corporate websites?

A: We photograph and retouch specifically for digital use. The files are optimized for both square (LinkedIn) and rectangular (website) formats.

Q: Do you offer retouching for skin texture and blemishes?

A: Yes. Professional retouching is included. We enhance skin tone and subtle imperfections while keeping you looking like yourself.

Q: How long until I receive my final headshots?

A: Turnaround is typically 3-5 business days for retouched headshots.

Q: Can I use the headshots for marketing and commercial purposes?

A: Yes. You own full commercial rights to your headshots. Use them on LinkedIn, your website, print materials, and any business applications.
