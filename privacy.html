Privacy Policy

Jordan Licon Photography collects only the information needed to respond to inquiries, schedule sessions, prepare proposals, deliver services, and communicate with clients.

Information collected

Inquiry forms and emails may include your name, email address, phone number, project details, timeline, budget range, location, and usage needs.

How information is used

Information is used to answer questions, prepare estimates, schedule photography or cinematography services, deliver project materials, and maintain client communication.

Sharing

Client information is not sold. Information may be shared only with trusted production partners when needed for a project, such as assistants, stylists, retouchers, makeup artists, or delivery platforms.

Contact

For privacy questions, email info@jordanliconphotography.com.
